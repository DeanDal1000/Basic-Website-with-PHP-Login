Alternative Treatments for Major Depression

If you don’t want to take antidepressants, due to the many side effects. They are many alternative treatments that can be applied.

What Alternative work?

  • Thinking about what you eat and drink – food and mood are linked, so you might be able to lift your spirits by changing the way you eat.

  • Exercise - Is a great way to reduce stress and anxiety.

  • Time - Sometimes waiting can be the only solution, as depression may eventually go away on it's own.


Though if you struggle to find other alternatives, you can talk to your doctor for recommendations.
