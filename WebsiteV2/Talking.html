Talking Treatments for Major Depression

There are many different talking treatments that can be effective in treating depression:

Cognitive behavioural therapy

Cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) is a type of talking treatment which focuses on how your thoughts, beliefs and attitudes affect your feelings and behaviour, and teaches you coping skills for dealing with different problems.


CBT allows you to work along with a therapist to recognise and challenge any negative thinking patterns and behaviour which may be causing your depression symptoms. This can help you change the way you feel about situations and enable you to treat your symptoms in the future.
