Site Guide

The site is used for gaining information and insight into major depression. To navigate throughout the website, use the menu at the top for a specific location where you want to go. The information page will allow you to view all the information on MDD. Click on the side menu to find the information you want to learn. If you highlight over the help, a features menu will appear. This will take you to the feature page that includes a self-assessment on depression. If you want a video tutorial click above.
